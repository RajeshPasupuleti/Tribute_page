Dr Norman Borlaug

The man who saved a billion lives

Here is a timeline of Dr Borlaugs life:

  • 1914 - Born in Cresco, Iowa
  • 1940s - Worked in Mexico developing high-yield wheat
  • 1960s - His innovations spread during the Green Revolution
  • 1970 - Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 2009 - Passed away at the age of 95

Dr. Borlaug's work is credited with saving over a billion people from starvation by improving agricultural techniques and sharing knowledge globally.
